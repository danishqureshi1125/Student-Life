Student Resources

Tools and support to help you succeed in college.

Academic Support

Use tutoring centers, office hours, and study groups to improve your understanding of course material.

Time Management

Use planners, calendars, and reminders to organize assignments, exams, and daily tasks.

Mental Health Support

Reach out to counselors, friends, or advisors when you feel overwhelmed or stressed.

Apps like Google Calendar, Notion, and Pomodoro timers help you stay focused and efficient.

Healthy Habits

Sleep, hydration, and regular breaks can improve focus and energy throughout the day.
