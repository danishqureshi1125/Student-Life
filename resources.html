Student Resources

Helpful tools and support ideas for students during the semester.

Academic Support

  • Use professor office hours when you are confused about assignments or exams.
  • Join study groups to review difficult material with classmates.
  • Use tutoring centers or academic support services when extra help is needed.

Time Management Tools

  • Use Google Calendar or a planner to track deadlines and exams.
  • Create weekly to-do lists to stay organized.
  • Break large assignments into smaller steps.

Wellness Resources

  • Take breaks during long study sessions.
  • Talk to someone you trust when you feel overwhelmed.
  • Build healthy habits like sleep, hydration, and exercise.

Productivity Apps

Helpful apps for students include Google Calendar, Microsoft To Do, Notion, Quizlet, and Pomodoro timers. These tools can help with planning, studying, and staying focused.
